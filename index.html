Welcome to X_ANCOHS Alumni

X_ANCOHS Alumni Association is dedicated to fostering a strong community among former students. Established in 2020, we aim to reconnect alumni with their roots and support the ongoing development of our beloved school.
2023 years
Since its establishment in 1993, X_ANCOHS has produced thousands of successful alumni who have made significant contributions to various fields, showcasing the impact of our educational programs.
5000 alumni
Our alumni network has grown to over 5, 000 members, providing a platform for mentorship and collaboration among former students.
200 events
We organize over 50 events annually, fostering connections and creating opportunities for alumni to engage with each other and the school.
